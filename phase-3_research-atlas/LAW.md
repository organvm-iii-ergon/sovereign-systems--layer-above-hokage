# LAW OF SOVEREIGN — The Constitutional Basis

## The Law

> **"If there's no data or statistics to prove whichever answer is the best one, then it's an opportunity for studying and figuring it out. So that means all of it."**

This is not a suggestion. This is the constitutional basis for Sovereign.

---

## Implications

| Statement | Consequence |
|---|---|
| No data → Study | Every knowledge gap becomes a research opportunity |
| No stats → Measure | Every assumption needs quantitative validation |
| "All of it" | No domain, claim, or path is excluded from study |
| "Best" requires proof | All execution paths need evidentiary support |

---

## Proof Requirements by Decision Type

### Level 1: Execution Decisions

| Decision | Evidence Required |
|---|---|
| Build Phase 1 (MVP vs Full-Stack) | Case studies, benchmarks |
| Build Phase 2 (Landing vs Pitch) | Conversion data, audience testing |
| Build Phase 3/4 (Atlas vs Dissertation) | Research ROI, time-to-insight |

### Level 2: Research Decisions

| Decision | Evidence Required |
|---|---|
| Causation claims | RCT or natural experiment |
| Attribution claims | Multi-touch model with validation |
| Interface claims | Retrieval metrics + human eval |

### Level 3: Meta-Decisions

| Decision | Evidence Required |
|---|---|
| Add a pillar | Gap analysis showing need |
| Change the law | Amendment process (see below) |
| Retire a study | Null findings + meta-analysis |

---

## Amendment Process

The law can be amended, but only through evidence:

1. **Proposal**: Document the proposed change
2. **Evidence**: Gather data on the change's impact
3. **Review**: Three-voice review (sympathetic, adversarial, orthogonal)
4. **Decision**: Human acceptance terminates the amendment
5. **Logging**: Update the law with justification

---

## Vacuum vs Gap Distinction

| Concept | Definition | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Vacuum** | Not yet discovered | "We don't know what we don't know" |
| **Gap** | Failure against known rule | "Should have X but doesn't" |

**Key**: Vacuum = scientific (unknown unknown); Gap = engineering (known unknown).

---

## The Evidentiary Threshold

```
EVIDENCE THRESHOLD = 0.05 (5%)

Below this → VACUUM (needs study)
Above this → ACTION (execute)
```

This is not arbitrary — it's the standard significance level.

## The Hard Anchor

The human's explicit acceptance or rejection **terminates** the reflexive loop.

This means:
- The law is always in effect
- Studies can end without conclusion (null findings)
- The human decides when to act vs study
